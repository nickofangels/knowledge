- **MCAS → MMP collagen degradation loop in hEDS** — Mast cells live in connective tissue; tryptase degrades ECM. Does MCAS accelerate the MMP-driven collagen degradation feedback loop documented in hEDS tendon mechanotransduction? Reduced/calcified pineal loses both melatonin AND NAS output. Supplementing melatonin only fixes half. HIOC (synthetic NAS derivative) exists but research-only. Pineal restoration strategy incomplete even with optimal melatonin dosing. What is current HIOC trial status? Any other NAS-TrkB agonists in pipeline?
